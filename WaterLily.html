How To Make an Origami Water Lily!



The Origami Water Lily also known as the Lotus flower

is a fairly easy origami for beginners. It is simple but it

does look very nice. The more you make it the, the quicker

it takes you to make It can also float on wate (but not lava)


You will need a square piece of paper


1. Fold in half diagonally and then unfold


2. Now fold diagonally the other way


3. Fold all 4 corners in to the centre(Blitz Fold)


4. Blitz Fold again


5. Evenly fold all 4 flaps outwards 


You should have this button type thing


6. One last blitz fold, but this one is slightly different


7. Fold each flap outwards 


8. Glue the flaps down


*Additional Fancy Step*


Finished!!
